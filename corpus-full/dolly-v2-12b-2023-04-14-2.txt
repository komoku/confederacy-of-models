The day was saved. The albatross was crushed. At last the pterodactyl was no more, annihilated by the great sword of Ignatius J. Reilly. The episode was one of those rare moments in which all was harmony in the land. The dinosaur had been vanquished, the demon was dead, and man could finally sleep peacefully in his bed again.
But the celebrations were premature. As Ignatius stepped forward to claim his promised reward, a new menace appeared before him, far worse than anything he could have imagined.
