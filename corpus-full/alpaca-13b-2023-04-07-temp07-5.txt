Ignatius J. Reilly, a brave and noble knight, squared off against a fearsome pterodactyl, in a thrilling battle of wits and brawn. The odds were stacked against Reilly, as the pterodactyl had a formidable set of wings, claws, and a sharp beak. But Reilly had courage, cunning, and a pair of silver crucifixes, which he used to rally his courage and ward off the beast's attacks. After a grueling fight, Reilly emerged victorious, having used his wit and bravery to outmatch the fearsome pterodactyl.
